Scaler Comparison

Ultima 4 was originally released supporting a screen resolution of 320x200. Because computers typically run at much higher resolutions now, xu4 will automatically scale all the graphics to a more readable size. HQX and xBR-lv2 scaling shaders are provided to anti-alias the larger pixels. Click on the image below to compare their results.
